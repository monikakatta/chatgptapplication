Chicken Recipes

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken in olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Grill until fully cooked.
3. Serve hot.

Pork Recipes

Roasted Pork

Ingredients:

  • Pork loin
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary

Instructions:

1. Rub pork loin with minced garlic and rosemary.
2. Roast in the oven until golden brown.
3. Slice and serve.

Veg Recipes

Vegetarian Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic powder

Instructions:

1. Stir fry vegetables in a pan with soy sauce and garlic powder.
2. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.
3. Serve over rice or noodles.
